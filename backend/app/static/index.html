How the Skyrocket score works

Each ticker gets a 0–100 score (50 = average) from a transparent model — no black box:

  • +18 Confirmed uptrend (price above 50d & 200d averages)
  • +12 Healthy momentum (RSI 45–60); +8 oversold (RSI <35)
  • +20 Analyst target implies >20% upside; +12 if >10%
  • +10 Earnings growth >15%; +8 Revenue growth >10%
  • +8 Near 52-week low (margin of safety); −8 near high
  • +10 Rule-based signal agrees (BUY); −10 if SELL

Grades: A elite · B strong · C watch · D weak · F avoid. Long-horizon view — not day-trading. When live data is unavailable, curated snapshots are shown and flagged.
